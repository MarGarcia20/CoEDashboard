09 · Nexti

Three gaps left to close.

The dashboard reads the real pipeline now. Three changes make it self-updating and complete the measurement system Sara will rely on for the resource conversation.

01

Populate BA Assigned Date this week.

Anu is assigning BAs Apr 24–25 to ready tickets based on Frank's sizing. With 4 items already classified at IT Prioritization, this week's assignments are the first real data point for the second half of the pipeline. Once 5–10 projects have both First Review and BA Assigned dates, we have a defensible velocity read on the handoff.

02

Require a status color on every portfolio item — Fridays.

Cycle-time data is strong; health-at-a-glance still isn't. Green / yellow / red every Friday, no exceptions. Complements the quantitative metrics with the qualitative read that lets Sara spot stalls before they become backlog. Today, 22 of 29 items have no color set.
