09 · Nexti

Two gaps left to close.

The dashboard reads the real pipeline now. Two changes make it self-updating and complete the measurement system Sara will rely on for the resource conversation.

01

Require a status color on every portfolio item — Fridays.

Cycle-time data is strong; health-at-a-glance still isn't. Green / yellow / red every Friday, no exceptions. Complements the quantitative metrics with the qualitative read that lets Sara spot stalls before they become backlog. Today, 29 of 37 items have no color set.

02

One-week time log for Mar & David.

Workload by count is imbalanced (19/18 — Mar is +6% this week). Workload by hours is not documented at all. A single Friday-retro question — "roughly, how did your week break down across intake, triage, project work, and comms?" — converts §08 from placeholder to ground truth. That's the prerequisite for the data-backed resource request Sara outlined.
